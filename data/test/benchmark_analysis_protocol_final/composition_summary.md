# Experiment 1: Benchmark Characterisation

The benchmark contains 100 total queries, with 70 dev examples and 30 test examples.

Validity-sensitive queries account for 92 of 100 benchmark items.
Attachment-dependent queries account for 5 of 100 benchmark items.
Queries needing local official docs account for 56 items, while external-only official doc needs account for 0.
Software-version queries account for 21 benchmark items.

The dev/test split retains the targeted subsets needed for later source-aware, validity-aware, and attachment-aware experiments.
